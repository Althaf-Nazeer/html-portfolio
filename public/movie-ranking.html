My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Avengers Infinity War

It is a superhero film directed by the Russo brothers and features an ensemble cast of beloved Marvel charactersincluding Iron Man, Captain America, Thor, Black Widow and many others. The film centres around the villainous Thanos, who seeks to collect all six Infinty Stones i order to achieve his goal of wiping out half of all life in the universe.

Titanic (1997)

James Cameron's epic romance/disaster film is one of the highest grossing movies of all time and has become a cultural touchstone, starring Leonardo DiCaprio and Kate Winslet.

The Dark Knight(2008)

Directed by Christopher Nolan and starring Christian Bale as Batman and Heath Ledger in his iconic performance as the Joker, this superhero film transcends the genre to explore themes of morality, chaos and heroism.
